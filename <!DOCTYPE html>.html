Exploring the Hidden Gems of Fire Island

Fire Island is a beautiful island known for its pristine beaches, vibrant communities, and unique natural beauty. In this adventure, we will explore some of the hidden gems that Fire Island has to offer.

Gallery: Fire Island

Explore the Adventure

Long Beach offers a variety of adventures for visitors to explore. From the serene beauty of the Sunken Forest to the historic charm of the Fire Island Lighthouse, there is something for everyone to enjoy.

This is the best place to get your mind off of work and spend quality time with your family and friends!

1. Sunken Forest

The Sunken Forest is a rare maritime holly forest that is partially submerged in water. It is a unique ecosystem that provides a habitat for various plant and animal species. Visitors can take a boardwalk trail through the forest to experience its beauty up close.

2. Fire Island Light House

The Fire Island Lighthouse serves as the tallest lighthouse in Long Beach. From here you can view the whole island in 360 degrees

3. Fire Island Beach

Fire Island Beach is a popular destination for beachgoers, offering soft sand, clear waters, and a relaxing atmosphere. It is an ideal spot for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying the coastal scenery.
